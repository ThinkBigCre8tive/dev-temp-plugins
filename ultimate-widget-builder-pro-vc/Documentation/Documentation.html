INSTALLATION & SETUP

Installation guide:

Follow these steps and see the resolute result within 1 minute.

  1. Download the plugin from CodeCanyon, unpack it and upload the [Ultimate Widget Builder Pro] folder to your wp-content/plugins/ directory.
  2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins‘ menu in WordPress.
  3. Go to your main WordPress left menu > Appearance > Widgets to drag and drop Widget Builder to your menu, then click Start editing to start builder your widget

Done! Enjoy!
